About Us

Located in Vibrant,Pedestrianised area,Cafe Nova is a bright and airy space with glazed frontage on two sides and an outside terrace.Serving our signature well travelled breakfast and all day food and drink menus, and freshly roasted coffee from our in house coffee roastery,our style is relaxed,open and friendly.Come for a coffee with the morning paper or a cocktail and small plates in the evening or anything in between!

Little touches like a live kitchen serving freshly cooked food (unlike the centralized kitchens of many others), offering home made tea and other international variants of the tea from almost 100 Best Teas of the world, European, Mediterranean, Italian, Mexican, Chinese cuisines, besides freshly baked Belgian Waffles and exotic coffees, Cafe Nova becomes the perfect place to stop the world a bit - and indulge your senses.
